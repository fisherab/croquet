Harwell Croquet Club

Golf Croquet 2010

Everyone plays everyone else in best of three non-handicapped 13 point games.

Please arrange your own games and e-mail the results to Jim (copied to Steve so that he can update the web).

The competition winner is the player with the most game wins, then, if necessary, the highest total hoop difference. In the event of a tie between two players, the winner of their game will be the competition winner.

All games should be played by Saturday September 4
